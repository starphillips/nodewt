About Us

Behind the idea.

Welcome to Our Website!

At Where To?, our mission is to create a space where events in Maastricht can be easily discovered, shared, and enjoyed. We’re just getting started, which means the site is waiting to be filled with upcoming events and local hotspots.

Maastricht is an amazing city, and we want to make it even better by connecting everyone — students, visitors, and residents alike. Whilst student life inspired this project, our platform is open to all.

That’s why we’re looking to work with organisers and venues who want their events to be seen and enjoyed by more people across Maastricht. If you’re involved in event organisation, we’d love to hear from you — together, we can make Where To? the go-to spot for discovering what’s happening in town.

And as we grow, we want the platform to be shaped by the people who use it. If there’s anything you’d like us to add or improve, let us know — your ideas matter.

A bit about us..

Hello guys, I’m Jonas, and currently in my third year of studying International Business at Maastricht Uni.

Another thing about me, I don’t like unorganised things. That’s why me and my wonderful girlfriend decided to create this platform to make it easier for everyone to find and locate cool events happening in Maastricht.

We are really happy to finally start that journey. We want to make it as easy as possible for people searching WHERE-TO go and for event holders to get word out easier, ensuring a fun and busy night.

Please enjoy, with more exciting things to come!

Jonas
