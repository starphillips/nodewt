Sankofa Nights

Dance. Connect. Sankofa.

How It Started

We're Oscar, Ben, Ferdinand, and Arthur, four friends who kept having the same conversation: "Why doesn't Maastricht have any parties with House music?”

We were craving those Afrohouse grooves and infectious Funkyhouse energy. We wanted nights where the music actually meant something, where people connected over rhythm instead of just standing around with drinks.

After months of talking about it, we finally thought: screw it, let's do it ourselves.

None of us had thrown events before. But we had passion, and honestly, that was enough. We took the leap, and Sankofa Nights was born.


Why Sankofa?

The name comes from the Akan people of Ghana. Sankofa means "go back and get it"—the idea that you honor the past while moving forward. There's this iconic bird that flies forward while looking back, carrying a precious egg.

When Oscar found it during a late-night deep dive, we all just knew. That's exactly what Afrohouse does—it takes ancestral African rhythms and fuses them with modern production. It respects tradition while creating something completely new.

That became our philosophy too: honor the roots, push the culture forward.


What We Stand For

Everyone's welcome. We've all felt like outsiders before, so we're obsessed with making sure nobody feels that way at our events. The music is rooted in African culture, but the experience? That's for anyone who appreciates it.

Quality over everything. We stress over every detail—the lineup, the sound, the lights, the vibe. We want you to leave thinking "that was incredible."

Community is everything. This isn't just about us throwing parties—it's about building something together. We want to integrate the community in every way possible, whether that's through collaborations, giving local talent a platform, or simply creating a space where everyone feels they have a voice and a place.


Come Dance With Us

What started as four friends wishing for something different has become a community of people who get it. We're still learning and growing, but we're doing this with our whole hearts.

Come as you are. Bring friends or come solo. Just come ready to feel something.

This isn't just our story anymore—it's ours together.

See you on the dancefloor!

Oscar, Ben, Ferdinand & Arthur
